🏗️👷‍♀️About Us👷‍♂️🏗️

Uhambo Olumyoli is a non-profit organization dedicated to building safe, sustainable infrastructure for rural communities across South Africa. Founded in 2023, we work hand-in-hand with local residents to construct homes, schools, clinics, and water facilities where they're needed most.

Our Aim

Our aim is to uplift rural communities by providing access to quality infrastructure that improves health, education, and economic opportunity. We believe every person deserves a safe place to live, learn, and grow - regardless of location or income.

What We Do

  • Rural Housing: Build cost-effective, weather-resistant homes for families living in informal settlements
  • Community Facilities: Construct schools, crèches, and community halls using local materials and labour
  • Water & Sanitation: Install boreholes, rainwater tanks, and sanitation systems to improve public health
  • Skills Training: Train local builders and artisans so communities can maintain projects long-term

Our Approach

We don't just build structures - we build capacity. Every project is community-led. We hire and train local workers, source materials locally where possible, and design with climate and culture in mind. Because lasting change only happens when communities own the solution.
